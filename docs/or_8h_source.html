447 /* here's how circ client-side purposes work:
448  * normal circuits are C_GENERAL.
449  * circuits that are c_introducing are either on their way to
450  * becoming open, or they are open and waiting for a
451  * suitable rendcirc before they send the intro.
452  * circuits that are c_introduce_ack_wait have sent the intro,
453  * but haven't gotten a response yet.
454  * circuits that are c_establish_rend are either on their way
455  * to becoming open, or they are open and have sent the
456  * establish_rendezvous cell but haven't received an ack.
457  * circuits that are c_rend_ready are open and have received a
458  * rend ack, but haven't heard from the service yet. if they have a

461  * circuits that are c_rend_ready_intro_acked are open, and
462  * some intro circ has sent its intro and received an ack.
